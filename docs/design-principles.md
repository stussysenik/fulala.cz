Based on these curated resources, here are the core knowledge areas and principles they collectively represent:

---

### **Core Knowledge Areas**

**1. Web Animation & Motion Design**
- **Theory + Implementation**: Principles of timing, easing, and physics-based motion using tools like Framer Motion
- **SVG Mastery**: Building performant, scalable animations from scratch with creative approaches
- **Micro-interactions**: Invisible details that make interfaces feel responsive and alive

**2. Design-Engineering Bridge**
- **Tactical UI Patterns**: Practical design patterns for developers (Refactoring UI) without deep color theory/art background
- **Designer's Engineering**: HTML/CSS fundamentals that help designers prototype and collaborate effectively
- **Systematic Thinking**: Treating UI as a system of reusable components and patterns

**3. Interface Quality & Craft**
- **Interaction Details**: Curated checklists covering input behavior, animation timing, and edge cases
- **Design Systems**: Maintaining consistency through guidelines (like Vercel's Geist system)
- **Zero-Bugs Culture**: Engineering quality as a cultural value, not just a QA phase

---

### **Key Principles**

1. **Details Make the Product**: Success is in micro-interactions and "invisible" design details users feel but don't consciously notice
2. **Practical Over Theoretical**: Focus on tactical, implementable patterns rather than abstract design theory
3. **Motion as Function**: Animation isn't decoration—it provides feedback, guides attention, and communicates state
4. **Cross-Disciplinary Literacy**: Designers benefit from engineering knowledge and vice versa
5. **Systematic Consistency**: Good interfaces scale through guidelines, not individual hero designs
6. **Quality is Cultural**: Maintaining high standards requires organizational commitment, not just individual effort

---

### **Implementation Philosophy**
These resources collectively advocate for a **craft-driven approach**—treating web interfaces as carefully engineered products where animation, interaction design, and visual consistency are core features, not afterthoughts.
